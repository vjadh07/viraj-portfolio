# Scenic asset prompts

All three scenic worlds were generated with the built-in image generation tool, then converted to optimized JPEG files for the website. The original PNG sources are preserved in `work/source-assets`.

## Sunroom Meadow

**Final website asset:** `public/assets/meadow-world.jpg`

Use case: stylized-concept. Wide landing-page scenic background for a paper-collage portfolio. A bright wildflower meadow beneath a cornflower-blue sky, with rolling green hills, restrained white clouds, small wildflowers, and distant hazy ridges. Sophisticated handmade editorial collage made from cut paper, screen-printed gouache, subtle halftone, and paper grain. Open left-center sky for readable copy, with the horizon in the lower third. Sunlit, optimistic, warm, polished, and adult. No text, logos, people, buildings, UI, childish styling, pixel art, or chaotic scrapbook clutter.

## High Country Postcard

**Final website asset:** `public/assets/alpine-world.jpg`

Use case: stylized-concept. Wide landing-page scenic background for a paper-collage portfolio. A graphic alpine panorama with layered mountain ridges, a cobalt valley lake, cool open sky, a long cloud bank, and sparse alpine grass. Sophisticated 1960s travel-poster collage interpreted through cut paper, screen-print ink, halftone, and paper fiber. Mountains rise on the right, leaving broad negative space on the left. Crisp morning air, confident, calm, and editorial. No text, logos, people, buildings, UI, childish styling, pixel art, or corporate illustration.

## Afterglow Garden

**Final website asset:** `public/assets/garden-sunset-world.jpg`

Use case: lighting and weather edit. Preserve the original garden composition, camera, geography, cut-paper construction, botanicals, lake, hills, clouds, tactile fibers, grain, and open right-center atmosphere. Shift the scene to a happy early-evening sunset after summer rain: lavender-blue upper sky, peach and coral light near the horizon, luminous cloud edges, a warm golden reflection across the lake, and lively teal foliage with subtle rosy edge light. Keep enough dusk-blue depth for pale paper cards to remain readable. Sophisticated editorial cut-paper collage with watercolor-gouache textures and subtle screenprint grain. Joyful, welcoming, intimate, dreamy, optimistic, and adult. No text, people, buildings, new objects, gothic mood, neon color, or oversaturated orange.

The original blue-hour version remains preserved as `public/assets/garden-world.jpg`; the site uses the warmer sunset edit.
