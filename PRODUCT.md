# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Design-conscious collaborators, hiring teams, studio leads, and curious peers who want to understand Viraj's technical work, clothing practice, and personality quickly while remembering the person behind the portfolio.

## Product Purpose

Present three connected sides of Viraj through one clear, scroll-led portfolio. Success means visitors can find relevant work quickly, understand the thinking behind it, and leave with a warm, specific sense of the person who made it.

## Brand Personality

Tactile, curious, and warm. The experience should feel authored, optimistic, artistic, and polished, with a calm editorial confidence rather than corporate formality.

## Anti-references

Generic technology portfolios, corporate landing pages, bento dashboards, glassmorphism, glowing gradients, futuristic interfaces, retro pixel games, childish scrapbooks, chaotic maximalism, and decorative collage elements that interfere with reading or navigation.

## Design Principles

1. Make the background a place and the paper a purposeful content system.
2. Let each landscape reveal a different side of the same person without fragmenting the experience.
3. Keep handmade imperfection controlled by a stable reading hierarchy.
4. Use details and motion to reward attention, never to compete with the work.
5. Preserve warmth and personality at every viewport size.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast for text and controls, keep keyboard focus visible, maintain 44px minimum interaction targets, provide semantic landmarks and useful alternative text, and ensure the full experience works with reduced motion, touch input, and without relying on color alone.
